CAMPUSES OF PSHS

Main Campus

Along Agham Road in Quezon City, Philippines, The Philippine Science High School-Main Campus, founded in 1964, stands as the gateway of the beginning of Philippine Science High School Journey.

Ilocos Region Campus

In 2003, the PSHS - Ilocos Region Campus was established, making its home in the charming town of San Ildefonso, Ilocos Sur. This campus, serving as the second addition to the Philippine Science High School System, has since been a hub of academic excellence and innovation in the heart of the Ilocos Region.

Cordillera Administrative Region Campus

The scenic Cordillera Administrative Region is home to one of the two campuses of the Philippine Science High School System, which opened in 2009. The school offers students an exceptional educational experience amidst the region's stunning scenery.

Central Luzon Campus

The Central Luzon site, the eleventh installment of the Philippine Science High School System, stands as a beacon for aspiring young minds passionate about science and mathematics, which opened in June 28, 2009 at Lily Hill St.,Clark Freeport Zone, Angeles City.

CALABARZON Region Campus

Calabarzon Region Campus is a campus of PSHS located in the heart of Calabarzon, specifically Brgy. Sampaga Batangas City , Batangas Philippines. PSHS-CALABARZON is the 14th campus of the Philippine Science High School System which started its groundbreaking ceremony on March 10, 2016.

Cagayan Valley Campus

Cagayan Valley Campus is the fifth campus of Philippine Science High School System which was founded in June 1998 at Masoc, Bayombong, Nueva Vizcaya.

Bicol Region Campus

Bicol Region Campus is a campus of PSHS founded in July 1998, serving as the seventh campus of the Philippine Science High School System. The campus is located at the heart of Camarines Sur at Tagongtong, Goa, Camarines Sur.

Western Visayas Campus

On July 1, 1992, the Philippine Science High School Western Visayas Campus was established, marking the founding of the third campus in the system. Nestled in Bito-on, Jaro, Iloilo City, this campus has been a cornerstone of academic excellence, providing a specialized education with a focus on science and technology.

MIMAROPA Region Campus

In April 2016, the MIMAROPA Region Campus was inaugurated, becoming a vital addition to the Philippine Science High School System. Located in Rizal, Odiongan, Romblon, this campus is committed to fostering scientific exploration and academic achievement in the MIMAROPA region.

Eastern Visayas Campus

The Eastern Visayas Campus, founded on February 1, 1992, stands as a testament to the early commitment to scientific education. Positioned in Pawing, Palo, Leyte, this campus has been a beacon for nurturing young minds passionate about science and mathematics since its establishment.

Central Visayas Campus

Nestled in the heart of the Central Visayas region, this campus, established on May 19, 2005, is a vibrant hub for scientific learning. Surrounded by the natural beauty of the region, it provides an inspiring environment for students to explore science and mathematics, preparing them for future challenges in research and innovation.

Central Mindanao Campus

Set against the backdrop of Central Mindanao's landscapes, the campus was founded in July 1998 , serves as a dynamic center for students passionate about science and technology. With its strategic location, it not only offers academic excellence but also connects students to the rich cultural and natural heritage of the region.

Southern Mindanao Campus

Embraced by the landscapes of Southern Mindanao, this campus, established in July 1988, stands as a leading institution dedicated to nurturing scientific curiosity. Located amidst the region's natural wonders, it provides students with an environment that fosters academic rigor and innovation, preparing them to be leaders in the scientific community.

SOCCKSARGEN Region Campus

Situated in the heart of the SOCCSKSARGEN region, this campus, founded on February 3, 2012 but opened on June 17, 2013, is committed to fostering excellence in science and mathematics. Its location enhances the learning experience, providing students with opportunities to explore and contribute to the scientific field within the unique context of the region.

Caraga Region Campus

In the heart of the Caraga region, the campus was established in 2013, serving as a beacon for aspiring scientists and mathematicians. Situated in Ampayon, Butuan City, the campus is surrounded by the region's natural beauty, it offers a nurturing environment for students to develop their skills, connecting their academic journey with the vibrant local culture.

Zamboanga Peninsula Region Campus

Positioned in the scenic Zamboanga Peninsula region, this campus, founded in 2015, is dedicated to providing a high-quality education in science and mathematics. The strategic location not only facilitates academic growth but also exposes students to the rich cultural and natural diversity of the region, preparing them for challenges in the scientific landscape.

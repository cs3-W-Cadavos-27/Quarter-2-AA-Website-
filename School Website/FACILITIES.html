FACILITIES OF PSHS CRC

Academic Building 1

The academic hub of the Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region, Academic Building 1, serves as the cornerstone of intellectual development for students in grades 7-10. Within its walls, vibrant classrooms foster a dynamic learning environment, complemented by essential facilities such as the Clinic, Registrar Office, and Guidance Office. It is here that aspiring Pisay scholars embark on a transformative journey, honing their skills and knowledge before advancing to the senior high program.

Academic Building 2

Academic Building 2 stands as the epicenter for the initiation and refinement of software and software systems. It is here that the innovative journey unfolds, with Computer Labs 1, 2, and 3 serving as dynamic spaces where students, spanning grades 7-12, delve into the intricacies of programming.

Academic Building 3

Academic Building 3 houses the essential STEM laboratory. Dedicated spaces for physics, chemistry, and biology laboratories are arranged, adorned with an array of sophisticated tools tailored for diverse experiments. This sanctum becomes the breeding ground where the scientific minds of scholars are not just developed but also ignited with inspiration. Here, the journey involves not only the mastery of laboratory tools but also the cultivation of a profound understanding of STEM education, enriching the skills and knowledge of future innovators.

Academic Building 4

Academic Building 4 holds the final chapters of the Pisay journey for grades 11-12 scholars. It's not just classrooms, but a place where teachers and staff units also have their offices, shaping the last years of learning and growth.

Guard House

The Guard House marks the starting point at the Philippine Science High School - Caraga Region Campus. It is where scholars and their belongings find a secure spot after school, symbolizing the beginning and end of each academic day.

Fabrication Laboratory

Nestled within this technological hub are the Fabrication Laboratory and AdTech rooms, designed to nurture the inventive spirit of scholars. These spaces not only encourage scientific exploration but also provide the essential tools for transforming ideas into realities.

Gym

The gymnasium. It serves as the heart of the school, hosting exercises, sports, and events within its spacious walls. It's a place where various activities unfold, creating warm memories for the school community.

Cafeteria

The cafeteria caters to scholars, providing snacks and lunch not only on school days but also on weekends. It plays a vital role in ensuring that scholars, especially those in dormitories, are well-fed and supported throughout their time at the school.

Student Activity Center

The Student Activity Center resonates with the music within scholars. With its ample space and a variety of instruments, it ensures that scholars' musical abilities and talents are not hidden but rather enhanced and showcased for the entire school community to appreciate.

Dormitory

The dormitory serves as a home away from home for scholars residing far from the campus. The separation of men and women dormitories ensures scholars' safety, and the presence of a dorm manager in each provides valuable adult guidance, creating a supportive environment for their academic journey.

Grandstand

The grandstand, with its open space and track field, hosts a variety of outdoor sports activities. It's a vibrant arena where scholars engage in sports, adding an energetic dimension to the overall campus experience.

Admin Building

The Admin Building is home to the offices of the staff, housing the dedicated individuals who play a crucial role in facilitating the overall learning experience for students. It stands as a hub where administrative support merges with the core of the educational journey.

STEM Park

The STEM Park, an after-school haven for students, captivates with its aesthetic beauty, instilling a sense of peace and creating meaningful memories for scholars who choose it as their post-class retreat.

Library

The library offers a serene haven for scholars, featuring an extensive collection of academic books in disciplines such as chemistry, science, math, and programming. Beyond textbooks, the library provides a diverse selection of novels for leisure, fostering a holistic educational experience. To support modern learning needs, multiple computers are available for research and programming, accommodating both dormers and scholars without personal electronic devices. This dynamic fusion of traditional and technological resources creates an environment where scholars can enrich their knowledge and thrive in their academic pursuits.
